Outreach

Wisconsin Prison Humanities Project

The Wisconsin Prison Humanities Project, formerly the Oakhill Humanities Project, is a volunteer program based out the University of Wisconsin -- Madison with the mission of providing a range of college-level courses to inmates in Wisconsin prisons.

I was invited to design and teach a new pilot course for the program, "Introduction to Digital Literacy", which would teach students how to use computers, mobile technology, and the internet in safe and productive ways. Click below to see the full course materials from out first semester.

Our first semester recently concluded successfully with a fully-enrolled class of 15 reporting that their apprehensiveness with computers has diminished and their comfort and excitement for technology has improved. We are excited to announce our return for the 2023 summer and fall terms with an expanded volunteer base and an anticipated full enrollment.
